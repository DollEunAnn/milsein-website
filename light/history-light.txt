Milsein Light Inc. was incorporated by Securities and Exchange Commission on February 11, 2019. This company is operated under Milsein Corporation.

In 2019, Milsein Light Inc. started partnership with Sassin International Electric Shanghai Co. Ltd. and Scheider Electric Low Voltage Products.

Milsein Light Inc. aims to offer expertise in supply and delivery of engineered electrical products to its continually growing client in the Philippines. We are determined to know the needs of the customers and provide a complete, reliable and efficient solution by offering the best brands in its area of expertise.

Our goal is to create a long-term business relationship with our clients by consistently demonstrate customer focus, pursuing excellence, quality of work and planning expertise designed to offer undeniable level of reliability and trust.
